ABOUT US

We are a small group hoping to venture into Humanitarian architecture as our goal of helping, inspiring and impacting lives in Kenya.

we are at small scale and inception process as we create awareness on human architecture but also venturing into helping people with reduced inequalities in the Kenyan society.

Our purpose is to plan, implement, monitor social and economic development programs and provide technical assistance to help communities. We intend to positively affect the different Kenyan Societies as i.e., improvement in health, nutrition and literacy status of the populations. We intend to visit individuals with difficult living conditions, homeless children, persons with disability, teenage mothers and single mothers.

Our goal is to change and impact lives without seeking any self-profit, fraud activities but as an act of goodwill and in the hope of growing into a nonprofit organization that tackles on the growing need for humanitarian architecture.
